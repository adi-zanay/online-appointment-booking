Covid-19 Protocols

Update : As you know, under the guidance of the CDC, mask protocols recently changed. We at Modify have decided to keep our mask policy in place. Even if you have been vaccinated, we are requiring all guests to continue to wear masks at this time. We will reassess as we move into summer and see what happens as everything starts fully opening up.

Measures we are taking for disinfecting our place

  • We are must using a disinfectant that is registered with the EPA for use in a hospital setting and is labeled as bactericidal, virucidal, and fungicidal.

  • We must using disinfectant according to the manufacturers’ instructions, which includes contact time, safety precautions, dilution requirements (if any), and proper disposal.

  • To disinfect a tool or equipment, we will fully submerge all surfaces of the tool or equipment, including handles, into the disinfectant in a covered container for the full contact time listed in the manufacturer’s directions. After disinfecting, the items must be rinsed and dried.

  • We will must wear gloves or use tongs to avoid direct skin contact with the disinfectant and observe all safety precautions in the manufacturer's directions.

  • Plastic or nylon capes and aprons may be washed in a machine and dried on any setting in a dryer or must be disinfected with a spray disinfectant.




About Us
Modify Hair Lounge is an intimate and welcoming six chair salon that focuses on attention to each individual client. Each stylist is an independent contractor which allows them to really focus on their personal clientele while having the ability to show their individual personality. Our goal is to provide a space with diversity in customers where they can feel comfortable to relax and unwind as well as catering to the promotion of a neighborhood community.

We welcome booking appointments with new customers by phone. Although we do accept walk-ins, it is always best to make an appointment in advance for best availability. If you already know your stylist, feel free to contact them directly to book an appointment. Individual service price lists, as well as contact information can be found on the stylist’s page.
